## Slide 2: Introduction
*   **The Problem:** Heart disease is a leading cause of death globally. Early detection is often difficult and expensive.

## Slide 5: Literature Survey & Dataset
*   **Traditional Methods:** Manual clinical scores (e.g., Framingham) often miss complex data patterns.
*   **Dataset:** Leveraging the **Cleveland Heart Disease Dataset** (UCI Repository).
    *   303 clinical records.
    *   14 parameters (Age, BP, Cholesterol, Max HR, etc.).
*   **Modern AI:** Research shows that Ensemble Learning (Random Forest) significantly outperforms traditional statistical methods in medical diagnostics.

---

## Slide 6: Proposed System vs. Existing
*   **Core Algorithm:** **Random Forest Classifier** (Ensemble of 100+ Decision Trees).
*   **Key Differences:**
    *   **Automation:** Manual vs. High-speed AI diagnostic.
    *   **Data Handling:** Linear models vs. Non-linear relationship mapping.
    *   **Granularity:** Binary (Yes/No) vs. Risk-Level (Low/Med/High) + Confidence Score.
*   **Engine:** Integrated recommendation system for personalized lifestyle advice.
